2D1N Yogyakarta Tour Package

A Perfect Blend of Culture, History, and Scenic Temples

Book Now

Tour Overview

Experience the best of Yogyakarta in this carefully curated 2-day tour package. Discover the rich Javanese culture, explore ancient temples, and immerse yourself in the royal heritage of this special region.

Itinerary

Day 1: Yogyakarta Cultural Discovery
Morning

Pick-up from airport or hotel

Our friendly guide will meet you at your arrival point in Yogyakarta.

09:00

Visit the Yogyakarta Palace (Kraton)

Explore the historical heart of the Sultanate and learn about Javanese royal traditions.

11:00

Explore Taman Sari Water Castle

Discover this beautiful former royal garden with its unique architecture.

12:30

Lunch at local restaurant

Enjoy authentic Javanese cuisine at a carefully selected local eatery.

14:30

Tour Prambanan Temple

Visit the largest Hindu temple complex in Indonesia, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

18:00

Check-in and rest at hotel

Relax at your comfortable 3-star accommodation.

Day 2: Borobudur Sunrise & Temple Tour
04:30

Early departure for Borobudur

Travel to Borobudur to experience the magical sunrise.

05:30

Sunrise at Borobudur Temple

Witness the breathtaking sunrise over this world-renowned UNESCO site.

08:00

Breakfast at hotel

Enjoy a delicious breakfast after your sunrise experience.

09:30

Visit Mendut Temple

Explore this small but historically significant Buddhist temple near Borobudur.

12:00

Lunch at local restaurant

Savor local flavors with a view of the surrounding countryside.

14:00

Return to hotel or airport transfer

Conclude your tour with transfer to your next destination.
